Frequently asked questions

What is AyahDay?
AyahDay is a free web app for daily Quranic reflection. It shows verses (ayat) with surah context in the translation language you choose.
Which translations does AyahDay use?
English uses Muhammad Asad (en.asad). Urdu uses Abul Ala Maududi (ur.maududi). Text is provided via Al Quran Cloud.
Is AyahDay free? Do I need an account?
AyahDay is free and does not require an account.
Where does the verse text come from?
Verses are loaded from the public Al Quran Cloud API using standard Quranic editions.
How do I share a verse?
Tap the share button to open WhatsApp with the current verse so you can send it to others.
Can I see a verse I viewed before?
Use the back control after moving forward. The app keeps a short history during your visit.
Does AyahDay show Arabic script for every verse?
The main reading view emphasizes translation. Surah names include Arabic; the body text follows your selected translation for readability.
Is AyahDay religious advice or fiqh?
No. AyahDay is for reflection only. For rulings or deep tafsir, consult qualified scholars and authoritative sources.

About AyahDay

AyahDay is your gateway to exploring the profound spiritual wisdom encapsulated within the Quran. Each interaction with the app – whether a simple refresh or a click – reveals a unique Ayah, complete with insightful commentary on its Surah. This app not only enriches your daily spiritual practice but also connects you to the timeless teachings of the Quran.

Immerse yourself in the enlightening verses (Ayat) from various chapters of the Quran, presented in both English and Urdu translations. Our carefully curated content provides a deep understanding of the context and meaning of each Ayah, making it accessible and relevant to our modern lives.

Designed for Muslims around the globe seeking a moment of peace and reflection in their day, AyahDay also extends its reach to anyone interested in the teachings of Islam and the Quran. Share the wisdom of these Quranic verses with friends and family effortlessly, fostering a sense of unity and spiritual growth.

Whether you are deepening your existing knowledge or embarking on a new journey of discovery, AyahDay serves as a constant companion in your spiritual journey, illuminating the path with the divine words of the Quran.
