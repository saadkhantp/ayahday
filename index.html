About Ayah Day

Ayah Day brings you a daily dose of spiritual wisdom from the Quran. Each refresh or click unveils a unique Ayah, coupled with insightful details about its Surah. Elevate your day with a moment of reflection and the option to share the wisdom with friends and family.

This app is dedicated to my parents, the roots of my life's tree.
